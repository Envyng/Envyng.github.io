The jobs could be anywhere in the UK but you will only be notified of jobs in your chosen areas. You have the option to choose the locations you wish to work in at any time.

Your first payment application is submitted no later than 7 days after you start, and you are then paid on a weekly basis directly in to your bank account.

You will get paid via bank transfer directly into your own bank account.

Your rate of pay will be agreed by you before you start. But the amounts you can choose from to be included in the rate is determined by current market rates according to geographic location and your current star rating.

Contractors post projects in your local area. If you are eligible to apply for the work, you will receive an SMS text message asking you to apply. If you choose to apply for the work, then you will be shortlisted to be selected for the work by the contractor.
You can improve your chances by consistently achieving high star ratings and by lowering your hourly rates.

You will essentially be working for First Finishes who liaise directly with contractors or sub-contractors. The jobs are commercial not domestic and once arriving on site you will be greeted by the project manager who will instruct you on where to begin.
