JAPANESE KNOTWEED IS A SERIOUS DANGER

Did you know that knotweed...

IS WORLD’S 100 WORST
INVASIVE SPECIES

Japanese knotweed is listed as one of the world's 100 worst invasive species and it can grow by up to 10cm a day.

IS AGAINST
THE LAW

Japanese knotweed isttt controlled waste which must be disposed of carefully so that it does not spread. It is against the law to dispose of it incorrectly.

RESULTS IN MORTGAGE
REJECTION

Mortgage lenders have been known to refuse mortgages on properties which are affected by Japanese knotweed.

CAUSES PROPERTY
DAMAGE

It can grow through wall, tarmac and concrete. It will find a weak spot and exploit it.
